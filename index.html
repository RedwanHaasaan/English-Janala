Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can begin by exploring our beginner-friendly lessons and vocabulary sections. Each lesson includes pronunciation, meaning, and examples to help you understand and practice English step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, the core features of this website are completely free. You can learn vocabulary, practice pronunciation, and explore lessons without paying any fee.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No, you can explore many lessons without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your progress, track learning, and access personalized features.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build vocabulary by practicing daily lessons, listening to word pronunciations, and reviewing example sentences. Regular practice will help you remember words faster and improve your speaking skills.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Currently, we focus on providing practical learning tools and vocabulary practice. Certificates may be introduced in future updates as we expand our course offerings.
